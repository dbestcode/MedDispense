 +------------+
 +   HOW TO   +
 +------------+

1) Scan or type the ID band or MAR BARCODE for your patient.

2) A PDF MAR will open, you can verifiy you orders here.

3) Scan all medications and supplies you want to use
      e.x. Take 3 pills, scan thepill bottle 3 times 
 
When you're finished with a patient type 'd' and hit enter
You may scan a new patient or press 'x' to close the Pixis
If this EMAR is closed it can be reopened on the desktop.

----------!!!NOTE!!!-----------
Only medications with have already been entered prior to your shift will apear in this MAR.
Orders given by phone will not be visible here.
